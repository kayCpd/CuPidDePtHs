My Theatre.. My Puppet

(My Theatre.. My Puppet)

My Theatre.. My Puppet.. My theatrical yo yo on strings. Your silly pranks.. Inspire and amuse me tremendously!

Strange stories producer.. My crazy script source.. Your energy is surprising.. Thanks for enhancing my creativity!

Sometimes I wonder.. How you do it..? But I have come to realise.. Never is there; a dull moment with you!

Unsurpassed skillful liar.. Consistent artful dodger.. Grand master of deceit.. Unrepentant two faced actor!

Your imaginary smartness.. Is my inspiration.. You think I am fooled..? Nay.. I am thrilled!

Titillated to bits.. What a scintillating experience.. As I write volumes.. From your astounding atrocities!
