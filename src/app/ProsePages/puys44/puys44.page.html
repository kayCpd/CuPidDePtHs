Pull Up Your Socks!

(Pull Up Your Socks!)

Make me.. He says. Tempt me.. He cries. Taste me.. He growls. Take me.. He pleads!

Like a roller coaster.. The journey begins.. Life’s challenge.. Is at it’s peak!

Don’t pick on me.. Pick me.. He begs.. If you so desire, tomorrow may be too late!

Today, he is basking.. In the euphoria, of the moment.. Don’t wait till tomorrow!

A totally different person, may emerge.. After he has counted, and taken stock!

Pull up your socks.. Make up your mind.. Do not vacillate.. Do what you have to do.

Grab the bull.. By the horn.. The game has begun.. A true Matador you will be!
