Percious Moment

(Percious Moment)

Quiet night with crickets chirping; Hey there, what are they doing? Must the night just pass them by?

The lovers need to be together! Do they want to waste this night? Will they rather ignore the vibes?

The Chrysanthemums have slept the Sunflowers are in slumberland! Not the Queen of the night she woos all with sensitive nostrils intensely erotic is her aroma captivating is her sweetness!

“Come my love”.. he says to her the night is calling.. Even the moon is crawling, calling us to cuddle!

“Methinks I will surrender”.. she replies and continues: “I’ll not fight this feeling anymore, no no; only me heart feels the pain as I struggle with my inner being”!

Where would you rather be.. Where the river bed is calm? Or where the waves of the sea have decided to rest for the night!

Alas there souls are weary.. The very depths of their beings wants an amalgamation; A cohesion of his soul with hers!
