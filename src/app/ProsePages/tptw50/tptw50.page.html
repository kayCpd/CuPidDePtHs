The Past.. The Whip!

(The Past.. The Whip!)

Caught up by emotions.. Overwrought by the past.. You often think.. And continue to think!

Hey.. You’ve got to move on.. You don’t win a race, by looking at your rear.. You must push on!

Don’t be pushed behind.. By bad reminiscences.. Thoughts of what should.. Or shouldn’t have happened!

It is done.. You cannot undo them.. Let them be.. Keep moving on!

When one door closes.. Leave it closed.. Open your eyes.. It’s a brand new day!

You cannot change the past.. You cannot rewrite it.. Learn from it.. Don’t yearn for it!

Bear the heat.. It’s gonna be for a while.. But time heals.. Better days are coming!

Time and again in nature.. A seed falls to the ground.. Disintegrates and eventually sprouts.. To give birth to a new plant!

The plant grows.. Gets nurtured by nature.. Flowers bloom.. And new fruits emerge!
