Living By The Memories

(Living By The Memories)

The hinge of the doorway to a lover’s heart, can only be oiled if it creaks rhythmically..

Resonating to.. The proposed partner’s heartbeat.. Then the portal of entry is made, to heavenly bliss!

Alas the stark earthly reality is that.. The song of love.. Is oftentimes not sung by two. Hum it anyway; enjoy it and live by the memories!
